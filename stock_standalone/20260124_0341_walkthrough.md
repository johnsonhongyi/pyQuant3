# Walkthrough - Price Gap Visualization & Hotlist Integration

I have refined the visualizer to include **infinite horizontal gap bands**, integrated automatic **gap detection with the hotlist/signal system**, and optimized the rendering performance.

## Key Changes

### 1. Infinite Gap Bands
- **Visuals**: Gaps now extend from the gap day **to the far right of the screen** (`len(data) + 500`).
- **Color**: **Bright Light Gray** (`#BEBEBE`, alpha 90) for higher contrast.

### 2. Hotlist & Auto-Follow Integration
- **Optimized Detection**: `_check_hotlist_patterns` now uses **vectorized scan** (Pandas) on the market snapshot to detect gaps efficiently.
- **Workflow**:
    1.  **Add to Hotlist**: Stocks with >0.5% gap are automatically added to the **"缺口跟踪" Group**.
    2.  **TradingHub Follow**: New signals are automatically added to the **Follow Queue** in `TradingHub`, enabling "auto-follow" mode.
    3.  **High-Priority Alerts**: Gap signals are pushed to `SignalMessageQueue` with high priority (Level 15-20), triggering UI flashes.

### 3. Rendering & Performance
- **Scan Limit**: Drawing scan is limited to the latest **20 trading days** to ensure smooth rendering.
- **Signal Types**: Added `GAP_UP` (Orange Red Arrow) and `GAP_DOWN` (Sky Blue Arrow) to visual configs.
